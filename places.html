Beautiful Places in Ghana

Wli Waterfalls

Wli Waterfalls, located in the Volta Region, is the highest waterfall in Ghana and West Africa. It's a must-visit natural attraction.

Lake Bosomtwe

Lake Bosomtwe, a meteorite-impact crater lake, is surrounded by lush forests and quaint villages. It's a serene and picturesque spot.
