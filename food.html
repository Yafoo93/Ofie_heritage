Food in Ghana

Jollof Rice

Jollof Rice is a popular West African dish made with rice, tomatoes, onions, and various spices. It's a staple in Ghanaian cuisine.

Banku and Tilapia

Banku is a fermented corn and cassava dough, often served with grilled tilapia and a spicy pepper sauce. It's a beloved dish in Ghana.
