Tourist Sites in Ghana

Cape Coast Castle

Cape Coast Castle is one of the most important historical sites in Ghana. It was used during the trans-Atlantic slave trade and is now a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Kakum National Park

Kakum National Park is famous for its canopy walkway, which provides a breathtaking view of the forest from above the treetops.
